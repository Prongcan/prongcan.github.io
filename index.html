VERA: Identifying and Leveraging Visual Evidence Retrieval Heads in Long-Context Understanding

Rongcan Pei, Huan Li, Fang Guo, Qi Zhu
arXiv Code

Through attention analysis, we identify specific Visual Evidence Retrieval (VER) Heads — a sparse, dynamic set of attention heads critical for locating visual cues during reasoning. We propose VERA, a training-free framework that detects model uncertainty to trigger explicit verbalization of visual evidence. VERA achieves 21.3% relative improvement on Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct and 20.1% on GLM-4.1V-Thinking across five benchmarks.

ExpInstructor: An Agentic AI Instructor for Experience-Driven Idea Evaluation

R. Pei*, F. Guo*, Q. Qi, Q. Zhu, Y. Luo, J. Yan, D. Zheng, Y. Zhang
Code & Prompts

We propose ExpInstructor, the first agentic AI instructor that emulates how human mentors leverage past scholarly experience to guide research ideation. ExpInstructor extracts experience triples from large-scale review corpora, constructs an Experience Graph, and employs an agentic reasoning framework for experience-driven idea evaluation. Both model-based and human assessments demonstrate that ExpInstructor significantly outperforms existing baselines.
